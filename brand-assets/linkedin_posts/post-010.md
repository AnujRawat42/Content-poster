Last night, during dinner my husband and I were talking about one of the businesses I have been working with.

I told him how I was excited to see the growth of this partner's SEO. He stopped me before I could go any further. "Heather, can I stop you? Everyday business owners have no idea what SEO means. I don't know what SEO means! Why do you always talk in acronyms?"

Touche! Every once in a while he's right.

This conversation made me realize something. Those of us in marketing throw around acronyms all day long, but business owners are busy running their business, not memorizing marketing terms.

What's important here is that business owners hear all of these daily, but no one explains them in plain English.

So what is SEO? SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is simply helping your business show up when someone searches Google for the products and services you offer.

So let's play a game, shall we? What's one marketing term you have heard and you've never really understood?
